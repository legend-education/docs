## How pages are structured

Every standard page on this site follows the same template so you always know where to look:

1. **Direct answer** — a 50–80 word summary of what the standard says.
2. **What it covers** — the scope in bullet points.
3. **Standards table** — official text, plain-English version, and student skill.
4. **Classroom examples** — how the standard shows up in real assignments.
5. **FAQ** — the questions teachers actually ask.
6. **Citations** — links to the official source so you can verify.
