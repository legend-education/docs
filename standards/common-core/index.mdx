The **Common Core State Standards (CCSS)** are a set of K–12 academic standards in English Language Arts (ELA) and Mathematics, adopted by most U.S. states.

## What it covers

- **English Language Arts**: Reading, Writing, Speaking & Listening, and Language strands across grade bands K–5, 6–8, and 9–12.
- **Mathematics**: Standards for Mathematical Practice plus content standards organized by grade (K–8) and conceptual category (high school).
