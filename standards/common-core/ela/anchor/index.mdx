# What are the Common Core ELA Anchor Standards?

<div class="answer-box">
The College and Career Readiness (CCR) anchor standards are the K-12 spine of Common Core ELA. Thirty-two anchors describe what every student should be able to do by the end of grade 12, organized into four strands: Reading (10), Writing (10), Speaking and Listening (6), and Language (6). Every grade-level standard from kindergarten through grade 12 ladders up to one anchor. Read together, an anchor and its grade-level standards trace a single skill across thirteen years of school.

Each grade-level standard, kindergarten through grade 12, ladders up to one anchor. The anchors and the grade-level standards together trace a single skill from age five to age eighteen.
