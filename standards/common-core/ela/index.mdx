## Strands

- **Reading** — Literature and Informational Text
- **Writing** — Argument, Informative/Explanatory, Narrative
- **Speaking & Listening** — Comprehension, Collaboration, Presentation
- **Language** — Conventions, Knowledge of Language, Vocabulary
