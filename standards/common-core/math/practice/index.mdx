## The 8 practice standards

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="MP1" href="/standards/common-core/math/practice/mp1">Make sense of problems and persevere in solving them</Card>
  <Card title="MP2" href="/standards/common-core/math/practice/mp2">Reason abstractly and quantitatively</Card>
  <Card title="MP3" href="/standards/common-core/math/practice/mp3">Construct viable arguments and critique the reasoning of others</Card>
  <Card title="MP4" href="/standards/common-core/math/practice/mp4">Model with mathematics</Card>
  <Card title="MP5" href="/standards/common-core/math/practice/mp5">Use appropriate tools strategically</Card>
  <Card title="MP6" href="/standards/common-core/math/practice/mp6">Attend to precision</Card>
  <Card title="MP7" href="/standards/common-core/math/practice/mp7">Look for and make use of structure</Card>
  <Card title="MP8" href="/standards/common-core/math/practice/mp8">Look for and express regularity in repeated reasoning</Card>
