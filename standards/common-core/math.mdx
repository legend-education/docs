The Common Core math standards have two parts:

1. **Standards for Mathematical Practice** — eight habits of mind that apply across all grades.
2. **Content standards** — organized by grade in K–8, and by conceptual category (Number & Quantity, Algebra, Functions, Modeling, Geometry, Statistics & Probability) in high school.
