The **Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS)** are K–12 science standards developed by 26 states and built around three dimensions:

1. **Science and Engineering Practices** — what scientists and engineers do.
2. **Crosscutting Concepts** — themes that span all sciences (patterns, cause and effect, systems, energy, etc.).
3. **Disciplinary Core Ideas** — the content of physical science, life science, earth and space science, and engineering.

Each standard ("performance expectation") combines all three dimensions.

## Grade bands

- **K–5** — grade by grade
- **6–8** — middle school topic bands
- **9–12** — high school topic bands (Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Earth & Space Science)
