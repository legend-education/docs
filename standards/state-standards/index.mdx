Most U.S. states publish their own learning standards. Many are adapted directly from Common Core or NGSS, but with state-specific naming, additions, or adjustments.

## What's coming

- A page for each state's official framework
- Explanations of how each state's standards differ from the national versions
- Direct links to the authoritative version on each state's department of education site
- Crosswalks from state codes to Common Core and NGSS where they exist
