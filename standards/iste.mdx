title: "ISTE Standards for Students"
description: "ISTE Standards describe the skills students need to thrive, learn, and lead in a digital world."
---

The **ISTE Standards for Students** describe seven roles students should grow into as digital learners:

1. Empowered Learner
2. Digital Citizen
3. Knowledge Constructor
4. Innovative Designer
5. Computational Thinker
6. Creative Communicator
7. Global Collaborator
