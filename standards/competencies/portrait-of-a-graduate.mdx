A Portrait of a Graduate is a district-level statement of the competencies a student should leave high school with. Districts usually publish a short list of competencies like critical thinking, communication, collaboration, problem-solving, and self-direction.

These aren't subject-area standards. They sit alongside them. Teachers are often asked to show how an assignment builds a Portrait of a Graduate competency on top of the academic standards it already meets.
