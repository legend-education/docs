Competency frameworks sit alongside academic standards. They describe the broader capabilities a student should have by graduation. Most lists include some mix of critical thinking, problem-solving, communication, and working with people.

## What's coming

- **Portrait of a Graduate.** District-level statements of the competencies students should leave high school with.
- **Skills for the Future.** Labor-market and future-ready frameworks like P21, OECD, WEF, and Hewlett Deeper Learning.
- Crosswalks between competency frameworks and the academic standards teachers already use.
