**CASEL** (Collaborative for Academic, Social, and Emotional Learning) defines five core competencies that schools develop across grade levels.

CASEL is a **framework**, not a curriculum or grade-by-grade standards document. Districts and states adapt it into their own SEL standards or embed it in existing academic instruction.

## The five competencies

1. **Self-awareness** — identifying emotions, recognizing strengths, growth mindset.
2. **Self-management** — regulating emotions, setting and working toward goals.
3. **Social awareness** — empathy, perspective-taking, appreciating diversity.
4. **Relationship skills** — communication, cooperation, conflict resolution.
5. **Responsible decision-making** — ethical reasoning, problem-solving, evaluating consequences.
