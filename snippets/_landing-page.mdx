## Standards by state

Every U.S. state, the District of Columbia, and the five inhabited U.S. territories publish their own academic standards. Many adopt Common Core or NGSS verbatim; others rebrand them; a few publish their own framework from scratch. This table maps each jurisdiction to the framework it uses for the four core subjects, plus the statewide and national tests its students sit for.
